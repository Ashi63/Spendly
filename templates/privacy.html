Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any notes you add. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply industry-standard measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. You may request a full export or permanent deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and analytics. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose beyond supporting the operation of Spendly. We do not integrate with social platforms or advertising networks.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
